Tendril is an AI customer-support agent that resolves tickets end to end for mid-market SaaS companies.

Today support teams add headcount linearly with ticket volume: a human reads the ticket, looks up the order in Zendesk, issues the refund in Stripe, and writes the reply. Tendril does all four. Across four enterprise design partners it resolves 62% of tickets with no human in the loop, cutting average handle time from 8 minutes to 40 seconds. We are at $1.4M ARR with 140% net revenue retention.

We price per resolved ticket, not per seat, so spend tracks value. Every action an agent takes is gated by an eval suite the customer owns and can edit, which keeps sensitive customer data behind their boundary and gives a regulated buyer the audit trail they need. The moat is the resolution workflow and the customer eval set, not the model: when an incumbent bolts a chatbot onto seat pricing it cannibalizes itself, and when a model provider ships a general assistant it does not own the refund-and-reconcile workflow of record.

Our first 25 buyers are heads of support at Series B to D SaaS companies. The activation event is the first fully autonomous resolution in their own queue.
